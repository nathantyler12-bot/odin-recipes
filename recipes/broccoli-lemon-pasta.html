Broccoli Lemon Pasta

A plate of broccoli lemon pasta. Credit: kamranaydinov on Freepik

Broccoli Lemon pasta is a really a blank canvas for a meal. It consists of three main ingredients: broccoli, lemon, and pasta. But there's a lot more you can do with it. It can be enjoyed on its own, or it can host many different types of protein. My wife and I are fond of adding smoked sausage or bacon and so that's included in this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1lb smoked sausage (or bacon)
  • 3-4 crowns of broccoli
  • 1 box of rotini (or penne) noodles
  • 1 lemon
  • Shredded mozzerella cheese
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Paprika
  • Chili Powder

Steps

  1. Prepare all the ingredients.
    • Slice the sausage into circular pieces
    • Break the broccoli down to florets
    • Zest the lemon, then slice in half and juice
    • Gather the spices for the broccoli
  2. Fill large pot with water and bring to boil. Don't forget to salt that water. As chef and author Samin Nosrat would say, "It should be as salty as the sea."
  3. While waiting for the water to boil, heat a skillet over medium heat and add some olive oil
  4. Cook the sausage until browned. Remove the sausage from the pan and set aside. Be sure to keep that fat from the pan in the pan (we'll use that for the broccoli)
  5. By now the water should be boiling. Add the noodles and cook to al dente
  6. While noodles are cooking, get the skillet hot again. Add the broccoli, a little more oil, and the spices. (A lot of recipes give you exact amounts of spices. I believe more in experimentation. Put the spices on the broccoli and let it cook some. Then taste and add more spice to balance as needed. If you go overboard, the acidity of the lemon that we add later will balance it out.) Cook until a little wilty and a vibrant green color.
  7. Drain the noodles and place back into the pot with some oil or butter.
  8. Add sausage and broccoli to the pot with the pasta. Add lemon zest, lemon juice, and mozzerella cheese to taste.
